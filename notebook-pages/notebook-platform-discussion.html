Notebook Platform Discussion
...

Goals
...

  • Easy to collaborate on multiple computers at once (with multiple accounts)
  • Great organization
  • Flexible enough for what we need, yet simple
  • Easy to print

Google Docs
...

Pros:
• Easy to collaborate
• All of us have experience with it
• Easy to print
Cons:
• Not very flexible
• Harder to organize more abstract data
• No third party embedding
• No code formatting

Google Slides
...

Pros:
• Easy to collaborate
• All of us have experience with it
• Easier to rearrange than google docs
• Allows for embedding of other media sources
• Vex has templates
Cons:
• Doesn’t have margins preset well
• No outlining

Notion
...

Pros:
• Easy text formatting
• Many embedding options
• Good printing
• Very fast collaboration
Cons:

  • Only Bryson has experience

OneNote
...

Pros:

  • experience from freshmen year
    Cons:
  • Too flexible (chaotic)
  • Subpar collaboration system
  • Printing is not great

Conclusion
...

We ultimately chose Notion for our engineering notebook for its collaboration features and organization system. At first look we were very impressed with its features and look forward to the flexibility it offers for us as our designs evolve.
