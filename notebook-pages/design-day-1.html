Drivetrain sketch - we want to use 4 wheels for the best chance of getting over the center bar in the middle of the field.


Here’s our flap design (early). We want to use pneumatics to deploy the flaps during the game because of their speed and the fact that we are hitting our motor limit.


A basic idea of what we want our launcher to do. We are thinking of cutting a section of the gear so that it slips and activates using the potential elastic energy in the rubber bands. This drawing is missing the bars standing straight up that hold the rubber bands holding the elastic energy.
